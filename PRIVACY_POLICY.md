# Privacy Policy for ALLAK Game

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## 1. Introduction

ALLAK ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") operates as a free mobile game available on Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, device ID, and unique identifiers
- **Usage Information**: Game statistics, scores, gameplay patterns, and session duration
- **Crash Data**: Error logs and crash reports to improve game stability
- **Advertising Data**: Advertising identifiers for personalized ads

### 2.2 Information You Provide
- **Player Names**: Custom names for multiplayer games (stored locally)
- **Game Progress**: Your game scores and achievements
- **Preferences**: Language, theme, and sound settings

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Game
- Monitor and analyze gameplay patterns
- Display personalized advertisements
- Fix bugs and improve game performance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraudulent activities

## 4. Advertising

ALLAK uses Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements. Google's advertising practices are governed by their own privacy policy. We do not have direct control over the data collected by Google Ads.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Your game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device
- **Security**: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store personal data on external servers (except for ad tracking by Google)

## 6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Mobile Ads**: For displaying advertisements
- **Google Play Services**: For app distribution and analytics

## 7. Permissions

The Game may request the following permissions:
- **Internet**: To download ads and game updates
- **Audio**: To provide sound effects and music
- **Device Information**: To optimize game performance

## 8. Children's Privacy

ALLAK does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete this information as quickly as possible.

## 9. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of personalized advertisements
- Disable data collection through your device settings

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:

**Email**: allak.game.support@gmail.com
